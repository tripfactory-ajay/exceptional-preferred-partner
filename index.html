What is the EPP Programme?
🤝
Independent Partnership
You run your own travel business — your premises, your staff, your brand alongside TripFactory’s. We provide the product, platform, and commercial infrastructure. You provide the customer relationships and sales expertise.

Real Commission — Not Token Fees
Revenue split starts at 80/20 (ORN/EPP) based on your verified Sales Base established during due diligence on-boarding. Achieve 1× growth → 75/25. Achieve 2× → 70/30. Paid twice monthly on the 15th and 30th.

Full Commercial Infrastructure
TripFactory’s booking platform, contracted hotel rates, MICE capability, visa support, and a named BDM. Everything you need to compete with the largest travel agencies in India from Day 1.

Mandatory professional development — destination modules, product training, sales skills, compliance. 12 modules per year minimum. Complete induction before your first booking.

Credit Facility Available

📈
Tier Advancement — Earn More
Commission split is tied to sales performance against your verified Sales Base. Opening split is 80/20. Hit 1× your base → 75/25. Hit 2× → 70/30. Every improvement is earned through measurable growth.

Additional EPP Terms
🔒 Key Obligations — All EPP Partners

The following obligations are binding on every EPP from the date of signing the EPP Agreement and remain in force for the duration of the Agreement and, where specified, beyond termination.

🔐
System Access & Login Security
System access credentials and login details are issued exclusively to the named EPP and are for their use only. Login credentials must not be shared with any third party — including staff, associates, or sub-agents — under any circumstances. Unauthorised sharing constitutes a material breach of the EPP Agreement and may result in immediate termination.
👥
No Sub-Agents Without Approval
The EPP may not appoint any sub-agents or representatives to act on their behalf or within their territory without obtaining prior written approval from ORN Vacations PTE. Any sub-agent appointment made without such approval is void and constitutes a breach of the EPP Agreement.
🔗
Exclusive to ORN Vacations PTE
The EPP works exclusively for ORN Vacations PTE in the capacity of Exceptional Preferred Partner. The EPP may not represent, promote, or operate as an agent for any competing travel business, tour operator, or holiday provider during the term of this Agreement without prior written consent from ORN Vacations PTE.
📋
Full Access to Company Records
Throughout the term of the EPP Agreement, ORN Vacations PTE shall have full access to all company records of the EPP, including financial statements, sales records, customer data, and marketing materials. This access underpins the ongoing due diligence and monitoring obligations of ORN Vacations PTE and the commission review process.
🔒
Data Protection & Confidentiality
All data collected or handled by the EPP in connection with ORN Vacations PTE — including customer data, pricing information, booking records, and proprietary business data — must be fully protected and must not be shared outside of the company or with any third party without the prior written approval of ORN Vacations PTE. All data handling must comply with applicable data protection law.
Territory & Area Rights
📍
Defined Territory
Each EPP is appointed to a defined geographic area at on-boarding. Territory and radius boundaries are documented in Schedule A of the EPP Agreement, taking into account population, sales infrastructure, and market opportunity.
🏁
Market Share Priority
Within your defined radius, ORN Vacations gives your EPP business first opportunity to serve leads and clients — enabling you to build considerable and dominant market share before any further appointments are made in that territory.
👥
No Sub-Agents Without Approval
Territory rights are held exclusively by the named EPP. Sub-agents or representatives may not be appointed within or beyond the defined area without prior written approval from ORN Vacations PTE. Area rights are conditional on active trading and meeting performance thresholds.
⚠ Area Rights Are Conditional — Not Absolute Exclusivity

ORN Vacations commits to giving the EPP first opportunity in their defined territory. This is not a guarantee of exclusivity. ORN Vacations retains the right to sell directly and appoint additional partners where demand cannot be serviced by the existing EPP.

Commission Payment Models
Model A — Split Commission
50% on deposit · 50% after departure · paid 15th & 30th each month
1
Customer pays deposit → TripFactory receives it → 50% of your total Commission paid on the next 15th or 30th.
2
Customer departs → Remaining 50% paid on the next 15th or 30th after departure date.
3
Statements issued on 14th and 29th each month. All payments by NEFT/RTGS. TDS deducted under Section 194H.
Worked Example
Total Commission on booking₹12,000
Deposit received 1 March₹6,000 → paid 15 March
Customer departs 20 April₹6,000 → paid 30 April
Best suited to

EPPs with strong deposit collection wanting early Commission flow on each booking.

Model B — Proportional Commission
Commission mirrors Customer payment receipts · paid 15th & 30th each month
1
Customer pays deposit (e.g. 30%) → you receive 30% of total Commission on next 15th or 30th.
2
Customer pays balance → remaining Commission paid on next 15th or 30th after balance received by TripFactory.
3
Full upfront payment = full Commission in one payment on the next scheduled date.
Worked Example
Package ₹1,00,000 · Commission ₹18,000
30% deposit received 5 March₹5,400 → paid 15 March
Balance received 10 April₹12,600 → paid 15 April
Best suited to

EPPs collecting staged Customer payments who want Commission to mirror actual cashflow received.

EPP Agreement — Gaps & New Agreement
❌
Gaps & Issues Identified
Items missing or needing amendment in the current draft
1. No Minimum Initial Period HIGH
EPP can currently exit on 90 days’ notice from Day 1. No protection for ORN Vacations’ onboarding investment. Fix: Add 12-month minimum lock-in.
2. Non-Solicitation Too Short HIGH
Currently only 12 months post-termination. Insufficient to protect ORN Vacations’ customer relationships. Fix: Extend to 24 months.
3. No Cross-Default Clause MEDIUM
Breach of any other ORN Vacations agreement by EPP does not currently constitute a breach of this Agreement. Fix: Add cross-default clause (Clause 25.10).
4. No Mutual Warranties MEDIUM
No dedicated warranties clause. Both parties should warrant legal capacity, licences held, no conflicting obligations, and no undisclosed insolvency proceedings. Fix: Add Clause 8.
5. LOI / Prior Agreement Not Preserved MEDIUM
Entire agreement clause currently supersedes all prior communications. Any signed LOI is wiped out. Fix: Add carve-out in Clause 25.1 for LOIs expressly stated to survive.
6. PIN Code Attribution Unclear LOW
Territory/PIN code attribution model not addressed. Could lead to future commission disputes. Fix: Clarify or retire in Schedule A.
7. Stamp Duty Not State-Specific LOW
Clause 25.7 says “adequate value” — correct but vague. Under-stamping renders agreement inadmissible in court. Fix: Create internal state-by-state Stamp Duty reference table.
